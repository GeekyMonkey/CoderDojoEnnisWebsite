Join the Club

Kids

Kids from 7 up can join. Only very basic reading skills are necessary. We’ll be splitting up the kids depending on their abilities. Once you’ve mastered one skillset, we’ll move onto a tougher one so that you’re always challenged and having fun. We don’t want anyone to be bored!

Due to large numbers this year, we are making a waiting list for the next intake of new kids. Please fill out this form to be put on the waiting list. When you’ve received an email invitation with the start date, you will be asked to fill in a registration form and you’ll be added to the member database.

Volunteers

Got a skill you think the kids would like to learn? We could certainly use some help. Email us at enniscoderdojo@gmail.com and let us know you’d like to become a mentor. We’re all volunteers though, so the only payment is a great feeling of accomplishment.

We can also use non-technical volunteers as well for the administrative tasks and supervision. Email us at enniscoderdojo@gmail.com and let us know that you’d like to get involved.

If you're interested in volunteering at our club, please send us a message on our Facebook account.
